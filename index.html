You will be given a randomly generated Target Number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your Total Score. The value of each crystal is randomly generated at the start of each game and is hidden from you until you click on it.


You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. You lose if your total score goes above the target number.
